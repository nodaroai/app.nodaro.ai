# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
Nodaro community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standard

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**. The full text —
including expected behavior, unacceptable behavior, enforcement
responsibilities, and the enforcement guidelines/ladder — is available at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating, you agree to uphold that standard.

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces (the repository, issues, pull
requests, discussions) and in public spaces when an individual is officially
representing the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported confidentially to the
project maintainers at **conduct@nodaro.ai**. All reports will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly, and the reporter's privacy and security will
be respected.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code and may take
appropriate, fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem
inappropriate, following the Contributor Covenant 2.1 enforcement guidelines.
