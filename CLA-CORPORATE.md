# Nodaro Corporate Contributor License Agreement ("CCLA")

Thank you for Your interest in contributing to Nodaro. To clarify the intellectual property license granted with Contributions from any corporate entity, Nodaro must have a signed Corporate Contributor License Agreement on file from each Corporation that wishes its Designated Employees to make Contributions to Nodaro.

This Agreement allows a Corporation (the entity defined below) to submit Contributions to Nodaro, to authorize Contributions submitted by its designated employees, and to grant copyright and patent licenses thereto. This is a legally binding document, so please read it carefully before agreeing to it.

If You are an individual contributor not employed by or contributing on behalf of a company, please use the Individual Contributor License Agreement ([`CLA.md`](./CLA.md)) instead.

## 1. Definitions

"Corporation" means the entity that is making this Agreement with Nodaro, as identified in the cla-assistant.io signature record.

"Contribution" means any original work of authorship, including any source code, documentation, design, configuration, test, or other content, including modifications or additions to an existing work, that is intentionally submitted by Corporation or by an employee, agent, or contractor of Corporation to Nodaro for inclusion in, or documentation of, any of the products owned or managed by Nodaro (the "Work"). For the purposes of this definition, "submitted" means the contribution is sent to Nodaro via a GitHub Pull Request, GitHub Issue, or another official communication channel designated by Nodaro and is intended for inclusion in the Work, but excluding any contribution that is conspicuously marked or otherwise designated in writing by Corporation as "Not a Contribution."

"Designated Employees" means the employees, agents, and contractors of Corporation who are authorized by Corporation to make Contributions on Corporation's behalf. Corporation may add or remove Designated Employees by notice to Nodaro at any time. Corporation acknowledges that Designated Employees may sign the Individual CLA in addition to this Corporate CLA.

## 7. Submissions on Behalf of Others

If a Contribution is not the original work of Corporation or a Designated Employee, Corporation may not submit it unless:

(a) Corporation has explicit permission from the original author to relicense the work under Nodaro's terms;
(b) The original work is licensed under a license compatible with Nodaro's dual-licensing model (e.g., MIT, Apache 2.0, or another permissive license); AND
(c) The Contribution is clearly marked with attribution to the original author, provides a URL to the original source and its license, and discloses any modifications.
