## Rebranding helper

A script is provided to rewrite Nodaro-specific URLs/branding to your own when
preparing a fork or white-label deployment:

- [`backend/scripts/rebrand-urls.ts`](./backend/scripts/rebrand-urls.ts)

Review its output and replace logos/wordmarks in the frontend assets as well.
